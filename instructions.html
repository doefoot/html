Ruiru Sub County and the Development Control Process.

Ruiru Sub County provides municipal services to a diverse population within jurisdiction of the now defunct Ruiru Municipal Its work includes urban planning and supervision of construction, environmental protection and improvement, regulating and ensuring high quality standards in construction and building materials.

Process Description

This service enables the customer to obtain a permit to carry out a project according to the proposed concept, where this request must include some or all of the following schemes: Architectural/Structural/Electrochemical...etc, where this service is mandatory. This service is provided by the Building and Works department.

Notes:

  1. Any person intended on undertaking any form of physical development is required by Planning Laws to first apply for and receive approval from the Local Government Authority.
  2. This request must be applied for all types of projects such as:
    • Extensions and alterations to existing buildings
    • New Buildings
    • Any change-of-use on existing buildings or premises
    • Buildings intended for demolition
    • Subdivision of land
    • Outdoor Advertising and signage
    • Earthworks, filling and clearing and
    • Regularization of existing developments

Customer Procedures and Documents

Procedure to Uploaded Required Documents

Make an online application for the service by registering and logging in and filling the Building permit Application form, and upload the following documents:

  1. Drawings (architectural, structural, mechanical, drainage …etc.) of scale suitable for examination.
  2. Submit all special approvals i.e. NOC (No Objection Certificate) from District Public Health Officer, EIA Report (If Applicable) etc.
  3. Proper Ownership documents (Tittle Deed, Lease, Share Certificate, Registered Sale Agreement, etc.
  4. Approved subdivision plan (if Applicable).
  5. Structural Drawings (All multi-storey buildings) of scale suitable for examination.
  6. Schedule of Structural Calculations ( All multi-storey buildings)
  7. A survey plan from Survey of Kenya 1:2500 (in the absence of an approved survey a beacon certificate duly signed by a Licensed Surveyor);
  8. Approval by Neighborhood welfare association (If applicable)
  9. Architects Professional Certification.
  10. Structural Engineer Professional Certification.
  11. Change of use approval ( If Applicable)

Receive Ruiru Sub County reply (Approve/ Reject/ Amend and Update missing data) within 4-7 working days starting from the submission date

Pay required fees and deposits Payment voucher, Notification (received from Online Service) with payable amount summary
